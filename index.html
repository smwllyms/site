  • Career in music

    Sam Williams (sqfx) has been producing music on his computer since the seventh grade, but his music career began very young. He picked up guitar lessons at the age of nine, started on the saxaphone at age 11 and began the french horn at age 15. Sam has also played the bass for a jazz combo for a couple years. Sam loves all types of music, but electronic dance has always been his favorite. From house to future bass, Sam has produced it all. He works primarily on SoundCloud, a free music hosting service.
